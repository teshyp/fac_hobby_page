Benefit 1

Positive side effects of meditation associated with mental health: increased awareness, clarity, compassion, and a sense of calm.

Benefit 2

When the body and mind are relaxed, the parasympathetic nervous system is stimulated, causing the body to stop releasing stress hormones. Many people who meditate regularly have learned to condition their body to relax on demand and can more effectively manage stress.

Benefit 3

According to research from the University of California, Davis, people who used generalized meditations programs have lower levels of cortisol.
